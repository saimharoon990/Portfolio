AquaWatch Pk

Startup Description

AquaWatch Pakistan | Climate and Water Intelligence Platform I am developing AquaWatch Pakistan, a data driven web platform focused on monitoring groundwater depletion and drought risk across Pakistan. The project integrates open satellite data from NASA GRACE with global water stress datasets to visualize invisible groundwater trends at the district level. Using interactive maps and dashboards, the platform translates complex scientific data into accessible insights for communities, researchers, and policymakers. The project also explores basic predictive modeling to estimate future water scarcity risks based on historical trends. AquaWatch Pakistan is designed as an open source initiative to support data driven decision making in water resource management and climate resilience.
